About Me

Who is Ahmet Ince?

Fortunately enough, I built a passion around computers in my adolescence and slowly progressed into creating applications but shamefully had no motivation to do so until the beginning of my study in University.

Luckily, i kept creating programs throughout that led to experiencing the feeling of success, worthiness and most importantly building the logical mindset and thinking.

Fast-forward 3 years, I've released a some-what decent personal website online and published a mobile app to Play store, I know these are rookie numbers however this was just the beginning. Consequently, i managed to secure my current job at YouLend as a Full-Stack Software Developer in which my skills and knowledge have increased exponentially within 2 years.

Hence i released 2 more mobile applications and redesigned this personal website twice (version 1 , version 2) that allowed me to develop even further.

So let's see what else life brings, and keep working on goals, stay focused and committed...
